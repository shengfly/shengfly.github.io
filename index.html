    I am working on Boston Children's Hospital, Harvard Medical School, as a Research Fellow for medical image analysis.

    I received PhD cum laude at Department of Artificial Intelligence, University of Groningen, on March 17, 2017. During my PhD study, I worked at the project of MPS-the Medieval Paleographic Scale. After that, I worked on a two-year Postdoc project "Mantis", in the Unviersity of Groningen. My research focuses on Machine Learning, Handwritten Recognition, Computer Vision, Pattern Recognition and Image Processing.

    I obtained my B. Eng. Degree and finished my master program in 2009 and 2012, respectively, both from school of automation, Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU), shaanxi, China, under the supervision of Professor Lei Guo and Professor Junwei Han. From July to December of 2012, I worked at IBM in Xi'an.
