I am an Assistant Professor of Electronic and Computer Engineering at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. Before joining HKUST, I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Stanford University, working with Prof. Lei Xing.

My research lies in the interdisciplinary areas of artificial intelligence and medical image analysis, aiming at advancing healthcare with machine intelligence. Specifically, I work on making machine learning more reliable, human-compatible and statistically rigorous, and am especially interested in applications in healthcare.

I am looking for self-motivated RA/Interns/PhD students. Feel free to drop me an email with your CV.
